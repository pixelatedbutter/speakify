About us

Speakify is an online language-learning platform designed to help learners explore and master new languages in a simple, fun, and interactive way.

We focus on popular global languages such as English, French and Japanese, making language learning accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience level.

Vision

To become a trusted and inspiring language-learning platform that empowers people around the world to communicate confidently and connect across cultures.



Mission

  • To provide accessible and engaging language-learning resources for learners of all levels

  • To encourage cultural understanding through language education

  • To help users build basic skills in English, French and Japanese

  • To make language learning enjoyable, interactive and effective
